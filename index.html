Rules

Guess whether the next card drawn will be Red or Black. Then, guess if the next card will be Higher or Lower than the first card. Now guess if the third card will be Inside (between) or Outside of the two first cards. Repeat for cards 4-6. If you mess up, you will be reset at Card 1.

Once you've complete one 'lap' (6 right in a row), you must now work your way back, doing the same order: Red or Black, Higher or Lower, In or Out, repeat. However, if you mess up at this half, you will be reset at card 6 (halfway through).

Watch out for the card count. If you run out of cards, you won't be able to continue anymore. If youre at card 10/12 and run out of cards, you'll be forced to restart...from the very beginning. Pressing the Reset button will also reshuffle the deck.

Note: Any ties are considered a loss. If you are guessing Higher/Lower, and the card drawn is the same as the previous card, it is considered a loss. If you are guessing In/Out and the card drawn has the same value as one of the previous cards, that is also a loss.
